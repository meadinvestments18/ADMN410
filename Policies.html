Financing

At Durham House of Pets we are as committed as you are to ensuring your pets happiness. We know that you cannot always get everything your like for your companions and feel financing is a great option for some. Upon Approval, we will provide up to $250 worth of financing. There is no interest if paid back in full in 6 months. To get more details and apply for credit, see an in store sales associate.

Delivery

We offer delivery on purchases of more than $250 and free delivery of purchases over $500. We will only deliver within a 25 mile radius. Please call or talk to a sales associate for availability.

Returns

Returns are accepted on non-food items only. Item must be in original packaging and not show any signs of use. The receipt must be shown at time of return to get the reimbursement. There will be NO returns without proof of original purchase.
